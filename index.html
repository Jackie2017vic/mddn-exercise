What's your stand?

There are always tons of information on internet. We often see one story with a couple of versions, what is the truth? What’s the story behind the scene? We used to what we see cause what we say. However, you cannot see all the things happen in person. We get used to listen to others’ story. Even if the people trust each other in reality, they hardly trust what you say on internet. The thing is we doubt everything on internet. Too many stories make people quiet confused.

On one hand, internet gives the voice from individual; on the other hand, the truth is hiding behind the internet.
